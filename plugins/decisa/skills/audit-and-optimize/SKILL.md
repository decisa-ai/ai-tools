# Audit & optimize — the advisor loop

This is the core advisory loop: surface what's wrong, explain *why*, propose a fix,
and route the fix through the reviewed changeset flow. Decisa is an advisor — it
proposes and explains; the human approves anything that spends money.

## 1. Surface the findings (read-only)

Pull from whichever signal fits the question:

- **`list_audit_reports`** — Decisa's rule-based account/campaign audits (priority-
  scored findings). Audits are generated by Decisa; you read the latest here, you do
  not "run" one on demand.
- **`get_meta_opportunity_score`** — Meta-side opportunity signal for an account.
- **Performance lenses** — `get_google_search_terms` (wasted queries),
  `get_google_auction_insights` (competitive pressure), and the ROAS truth
  (`get_campaign_roas`, `get_roas_discrepancy`), plus `get_meta_insights`.

## 2. Explain before acting

- **`explain_recommendation`** — the reasoning + evidence behind a recommendation.
  Always present this to the user; never propose a money change as a black box.
- **`get_recommendation_acceptance`** — shown/accepted/dismissed history, useful to
  avoid re-pitching something already dismissed.

Sanity-check against the `roas-investigation` skill first: a "bad" campaign with a
low attribution match rate is a *tracking* problem, not an optimization target.
Don't draft a spend cut against mis-tracked data.

## 3. Draft the fix as a changeset (never apply blind)

Every fix that spends money or mutates platform state goes through the changeset
flow (see `changeset-safety`). Typical fixes:

- **Cut waste** — `add_google_negative_keywords` for junk search terms;
  `pause_google_keywords` for non-converters.
- **Reallocate / pause** — `update_campaign_budget`, `pause_campaign`.
- Bundle related fixes with `create_bulk_changeset`; single fix with
  `create_changeset`.

Then `get_changeset` → show the user the diff + estimated spend impact →
`submit_changeset` → `approve_changeset` → `apply_changeset`. Re-read with
`get_changeset` to confirm `APPLIED` and surface any per-item `FAILED`.

## The loop, in one line

`list_audit_reports` / opportunity + performance signals → `explain_recommendation`
→ draft via `create_changeset` → review → apply. Findings without a previewed,
approved fix are just noise; a fix applied without explanation breaks trust.

## Guardrails

- **Prioritize by impact** — lead with the highest-priority finding and its money
  effect, not a wall of every issue.
- **One reasoned proposal at a time** beats a bulk dump the user can't evaluate.
- **Quantify** — "this campaign returns $0.40 per $1; pausing saves ~$X/day" — so
  the user decides with numbers, not vibes.
